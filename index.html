The students, employees, and alumni of Southern Adventist University have a long tradition of serving others.
Whether it's handing out warm clothes and sandwiches to the homeless in Chattanooga, providing free health clinics in Central America, or tutoring struggling middle-schoolers in your hometown, each moment makes a difference and brings a little light to the world.
As we mark Southern's 125th anniversary, we want to honor that tradition by collecting your hours of service, aiming to reach a collective total of 125,000 by the end of the year.
